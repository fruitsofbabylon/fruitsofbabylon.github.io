About Me

I am a junior frontend developer currently based in London, UK. Starting with an Information Technology bachelor degree mostly based on networking and server environment, I quickly switched to the software area of computer science - frontend development. During my web development experience, I worked on display advertisements and websites using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

A combination of logic and visual creativity in the website development process daily brings me joy and inspiration. I try to focus on accessibility and performance to provide a user-friendly experience.

Apart from coding, I’m into analog photography (both film and polaroid) and consuming non-fiction materials like books, documentaries, etc.
